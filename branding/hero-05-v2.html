What changed vs v1: removed the bottom reverse-scrolling ticker tape. In its place sits a real 7-day catalyst calendar with typed events (PDUFA, AdCom, Readout, Earnings) — the event types themselves are biotech-specific and would never appear on a generic finance site.

New: Sector pulse strip. Four stats that combine clinical signal (Ph3 readouts tracked), regulatory pipeline (PDUFA dates, FDA approvals YTD), and market performance (XBI index). This is the "biotech × finance" combo made explicit — a Yahoo Finance would never show you Ph3 readout counts or PDUFA backlog, and a pure trial tracker would never show you the index performance. Together they say this product speaks both languages.
