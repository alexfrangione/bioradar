Most viruses (adenoviruses, polio, HIV core) are actually icosahedral — 20 triangular faces. This one shows the hexagonal profile with the internal triangulation lines that suggest the 3D structure. Geometric, calm, looks architectural. Least "spiky ball," most "crystalline capsid."
